Look Mom I'm Famous

I like to believe that I have a constant appreciation for the beauty around me. It doesn't take an expensive camera or, really, any equipment to capture the world as you wish everyone could see it. If there's one thing I have learned through an appreciation for the arts, and especially for cinema, it's that framing is everything. We often think about backgrounds as complementary to the subject. But without a background, there is no foreground.


What lives behind every image is equally as important as the image itself. Whether it be the pastel skies at sunset, the dumbass look on my friend's faces, or mere blades of grass; meaning is everywhere. And perspective is everything.


As a gift to you, the viewer of my first ever website with substance, here are some of my proudest, raw, unedited photographs. These were years in the capturing, all *Shot on iPhone*, and each representing a multitude of adventures that I have found myself in over the years. Thank you, enjoy..
